Buying a Travel Card

Travel is your ticket to travel on buses in Darwin and many parts of regional Northern Territory.

Buy your Travel Card, top up with Travel Card Money or travel card Pass and you're ready to go.

Topping up to travel

If you travel occasionally, pay as you go with travel card Money. If you travel often, buy consecutive days with a travel card Pass.

Your travel card can hold both travel card Money and travel card Pass. Compare and choose the best one for you each time you top up.

Managing your travel card

Register your travel card online to enjoy special benefits.

See your travel history, set Auto Top Up, protect your travel card balance and receive a new card when yours expires.
