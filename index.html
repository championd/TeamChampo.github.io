Get Card

You can buy weekly or multiple trip Tap and Ride cards.
The full fare card is green and the concession card is orange. Both are rechargeable.
You can't use your travel card to pay another person's fare at the same time as your own. Each person travelling will need to purchase their own card.

Tap and Ride cards are issued for multiple and weekly trips.
Concession card holders can get discounted travel.



Top up Your tap and ride

You will need to have:
Your tap and ride Card
Your Australian-issued Mastercard or Visa credit/debit card
